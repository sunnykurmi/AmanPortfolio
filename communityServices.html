Community-Services

Interned at Bhuvaneshwari Mahila Ashram

Since 2022, I have volunteered as an Assistant Project Manager at Bhuvaneshwari Mahila Ashram (an NGO), where I contribute to solid waste management, revenue generation, and recycling education. I collected over 50 kg of waste blades from salons and assisted in a project that involved creating bricks from plastic waste, initiating quality control measures in collaboration with the Department of Civil Engineering at IIT Roorkee. My work focuses on promoting sustainable practices and raising awareness about environmental issues within the community.

MY SSF

I was selected for the Swachhta Saarthi Fellowship Category A in 2022 by the Office of the Principal Scientific Advisor (PSA), Government of India, and received a grant of INR 6,000. This one-year program focused on environmental sustainability and involved several initiatives, including the preparation and distribution of seed bombs in open land to promote green cover, e-waste collection drives in the school locality to raise awareness about proper disposal, and the preparation of compost pits to encourage organic waste management. I also organized awareness workshops targeting the public and youth to educate them about sustainable practices, waste segregation, and the importance of community involvement in environmental conservation. Through these efforts, I aimed to inspire positive change and foster a sense of responsibility toward our environment within the community.

Taught English Communication

I volunteered 75 hours tutoring English to Grade 6-8 students in a government school, teaching them English communication in an engaging and fun way. I utilized interactive activities, games, and creative exercises to make learning enjoyable, helping students build their confidence and improve their language skills. My approach focused on practical usage, encouraging them to express themselves and participate actively in discussions.

WeVOIS as a state ambassador

I led a collaborative e-waste collection and recycling project with WeVOIS, a Jaipur-based startup, as a State Ambassador, furthering my involvement in the Swachhta Saarthi Fellowship program. During the e-waste collection drive, we successfully collected 250 kg of e-waste, raising awareness about the importance of proper electronic waste disposal and recycling. It contributed to environmental sustainability but also engaged the community in responsible waste management practices. By organizing workshops and outreach activities, I aimed to educate participants on the dangers of e-waste and promote sustainable solutions for managing electronic waste effectively.
